Yet Another Bread Pudding Recipe

There aren’t enough bread pudding recipes on the internet. This one is mine! Ingredients 1 loaf of stale bread (ie; 1-2 day old baguette or Italian bread, 1-2 day old brioche) 500ml (2.25 cups) whole milk 3 eggs 198g (1 cup) white granulated or caster sugar 28g (2 tbsp) butter plus extra for greasing the pan 1/2 tsp sea salt 2 tsp ground cinnamon 1/2 tsp allspice (can substitute nutmeg, or omit if not available) 1 vanilla bean (or 1 tsp vanilla extract) Equipment 8x8 inch (20x20 cm) baking dish (a 9x9 inch disk also works) medium sided glass or ceramic bowl medium sized sauce pot Instructions If you don’t have stale bread on hand many bakeries, patissieres, and bahn mi shops will sell day old loaves at a discount.
